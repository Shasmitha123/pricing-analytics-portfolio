================================================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This project simulates the work of a Pricing Strategy Analyst responsible for
evaluating the commercial performance of a B2B SaaS company. Using a dataset of
638 sales opportunities, the project assesses pricing effectiveness, validates
pricing policy compliance, analyzes product and customer performance, identifies
pricing optimization opportunities, and develops executive recommendations.

The project follows a complete commercial pricing analytics workflow beginning
with raw opportunity-level data validation and ending with executive reporting
through an interactive business intelligence dashboard.

================================================================================
BUSINESS OBJECTIVE
================================================================================

The objective of this project was to evaluate whether the company's pricing
strategy effectively balances revenue growth, customer acquisition, and
profitability while remaining compliant with internal pricing policies.

The analysis answers the following business questions:

• Are pricing calculations internally consistent?
• Are pricing policies being followed?
• Which products contribute the greatest commercial value?
• How does customer lifecycle affect commercial performance?
• Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
• Where do pricing optimization opportunities exist?
• What strategic actions should leadership consider?

================================================================================
PROJECT GOALS
================================================================================

1. Validate pricing calculations and financial metrics.
2. Audit pricing policy compliance.
3. Evaluate commercial performance across products.
4. Compare performance across customer lifecycle stages.
5. Analyze strategic customer segments.
6. Assess pricing optimization opportunities.
7. Present executive-ready business recommendations.

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

1.

Enterprise Suite generated approximately 48% of total company revenue while
receiving the lowest average approved discount, indicating strong pricing
power.

------------------------------------------------------------

2.

Renewal and Expansion opportunities consistently outperformed New Business
opportunities, suggesting significantly higher commercial success among
existing customers.

------------------------------------------------------------

3.

Enterprise customers within the Legal and Technology industries represented
the company's highest-value strategic customer segments.

------------------------------------------------------------

4.

Nexus AI Assistant produced the weakest commercial performance despite
receiving discounts comparable to stronger-performing products, suggesting
that pricing alone is unlikely to improve outcomes.

------------------------------------------------------------

5.

Nexus Insights achieved the highest win rate and highest gross margin,
creating an opportunity to test reduced discount levels while maintaining
commercial performance.

================================================================================
EXECUTIVE RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

• Protect Enterprise Suite pricing discipline.

• Review Nexus AI Assistant pricing strategy and value proposition.

• Test lower discount levels for Nexus Insights.

• Prioritize Enterprise customers within Legal and Technology industries.

• Investigate non-pricing factors affecting lower-performing customer segments.
